Title: What Is a Random Variable?
Date: 2025-01-01
Category: Blog
Tags: basics, probability

A random variable is a rule that assigns a number to each outcome of a random experiment.

When you roll a fair die, let X be the number rolled: 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, or 6.

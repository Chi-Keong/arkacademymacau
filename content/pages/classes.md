Title: Classes

At Ark Academy Macau, all classes are:

- Small group (up to 6 students)
- Focused on understanding, not memorising
- Built around real exam-style questions and real data

## Planned courses

### 1. Foundations of Statistics
- Descriptive statistics
- Probability basics
- Random variables and distributions
- Intro to hypothesis testing

### 2. Applied Statistics with Examples
- Confidence intervals
- Hypothesis testing in detail
- Regression basics
- Interpreting results in plain language

### 3. Statistics Support / Tutoring
One-to-one or small-group support based on your university syllabus.
